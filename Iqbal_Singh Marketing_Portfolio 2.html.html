About Anaya

Craft that
breathes heritage.

Anaya Ethnic Wears is a celebration of India's rich heritage and contemporary style. Our collections blend traditional craftsmanship with modern silhouettes to create outfits that are elegant, timeless and made for every celebration.

We source directly from master weavers — Banarasi silk from Varanasi, Chanderi from Madhya Pradesh, Bandhani from Kutch. Every thread carries a story.

Our Heritage

Rooted in Bharat.
Worn with Pride.

Each garment at Anaya is a confluence of centuries-old craft and contemporary sensibility — handwoven, hand-embroidered, and made to be passed down through generations.

We do not manufacture. We commission art. Each piece takes weeks to complete, shaped by hands that have learned their craft across generations.
